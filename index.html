Suhiel Ezael

Suhiel is a young short lithe girl who's quite scrawny, she has unkempt hair and ragged worn clothes, obvious signs of homelessness and a lack of a proper source of food. Her every movement oozes caution and she flinches at the slightest sounds she hears, her eyes quickly dart from object to object, from person to person- and her hands and legs always seem to be moving or fidgeting in some way.
